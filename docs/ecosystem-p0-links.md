## UTM convention

- `utm_source`: the platform sending the visitor
- `utm_medium`: `organic_social`, `shorts`, `profile`, `pin`, or `marketplace`
- `utm_campaign`: the durable campaign or destination name
- `utm_content`: optional creative or placement name

Do not add UTMs to links between pages on gomisgoldendays.com. Internal UTMs would overwrite the original acquisition source.
